Large PDFs are supported (processed locally).

How to use

  1. Drop a PDF or click Choose PDF.
  2. Use the checkboxes to select pages. Drag a page row (or use ↑ ↓ ↤ ↦) to change order.
  3. Choose an Export mode and click Export.

Tips

  • Thumbnails are rendered on the fly — big PDFs may take a moment.
  • “Split all” ignores the page checkboxes and exports every page.
  • If a file is encrypted, it can’t be opened in the browser.
