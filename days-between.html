How it works
  • Exclusive (checkbox off): counts whole days between start and end.
  • Inclusive (checkbox on): adds 1 day to also count the start (and end) day.
  • Full weeks: complete 7-day blocks inside the range.
  • Leftover days: remaining days after removing all full weeks.
