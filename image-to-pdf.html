How to use

  1. Drop images or click Choose Images.
  2. Select Page Size, Margins, and Fit Mode.
  3. Reorder with ↑ ↓, then click Create PDF.

Tips

  • Fit keeps the whole image; Fill fills the page (may crop).
  • Mix portrait & landscape — pages auto-rotate.
  • Need smaller size? Export PDF → Images, then re-PDF with lower quality.
